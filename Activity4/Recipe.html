Delicious Biryani

Ingredients

  • 700g (1.5 lbs) bone-in chicken thighs and drumsticks
  • 2/3 cup (165ml) plain, fresh yogurt or curd
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala or biryani masala
  • 1.5 cups (300g) aged basmati rice

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in a mixture of yogurt and spices, such as garam masala, ginger, and garlic paste.
  2. Prepare fried onions (birista) by thinly slicing onions and cooking them in ghee until golden brown. Set them aside for layering and garnish.
  3. Add finishing touches by drizzling saffron-infused milk and melted ghee over the rice.
  4. 1Cook on "dum" by sealing the pot with a tight-fitting lid or foil and cooking on very low heat for 20 minutes.
  5. Rest and serve by turning off the heat and letting it rest for another 10–15 minutes before gently fluffing the rice and serving

Enjoy your homemade biryani!
